## Response Process

We aim to:
- Acknowledge the report within **5 working days**
- Assess the issue and determine its severity
- Apply a fix where appropriate within the next development iteration

As this is an academic project, fixes will be released on a **best-effort basis**.

---

## Responsible Disclosure

We kindly request that you follow responsible disclosure practices and avoid publicly disclosing the vulnerability until it has been reviewed and addressed.
